# 📸 Dashboard Preview

## Sales & Inventory MIS Dashboard
![Dashboard](Screenshots/Sales_Inventory_Dashboard_Page.png)

### Dashboard Overview

The **Sales & Inventory MIS Dashboard** provides a real-time executive view of retail business performance by combining sales, profitability, and inventory metrics into a single interactive report. It enables business users to monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), analyze sales trends, compare regional performance, identify top-selling products, and detect inventory risks. Interactive slicers allow users to filter the dashboard by **Date**, **Category**, and **Region** for detailed analysis.

---

### Dashboard Components

#### 🔹 KPI Cards
The KPI section highlights the most important business metrics at a glance.

- **Total Profit** – Displays overall profit generated from sales.
- **Total Revenue** – Shows total revenue earned during the selected period.
- **Low Stock Products** – Identifies products that require immediate replenishment.
- **Profit Margin (%)** – Displays the profitability percentage of total sales.

---

#### 🔹 Interactive Filters

Users can dynamically filter the dashboard using:

- 📅 Date
- 🏷️ Category
- 🌍 Region

These filters update all visuals simultaneously to provide focused business insights.

---

#### 🔹 Sales Over Time

A line chart visualizing monthly sales trends, enabling users to identify seasonal demand patterns, growth trends, and performance fluctuations over time.

---

#### 🔹 Sales by Region

A clustered column chart comparing sales across different regions, helping identify the highest and lowest-performing markets.

---

#### 🔹 Profit by Category

A donut chart illustrating the contribution of each product category to overall profit, making it easier to identify the most profitable product segments.

---

#### 🔹 Top 10 Product Sales

A horizontal bar chart ranking the highest-selling products based on revenue, allowing quick identification of best-performing products.

---

#### 🔹 Inventory Status

A stacked bar chart showing the inventory distribution of products, including:

- In Stock
- Current Stock
- Stock Out

This visual helps identify inventory shortages and supports stock replenishment planning.

---

#### 🔹 Executive Business Insight

A dynamic insight section summarizes key findings from the dashboard, helping decision-makers quickly understand business performance and prioritize actions.


> **Executive Insight:** Sales performance is led by the East region and Product_71, while Furniture contributes the highest profitability. Low-stock products require immediate replenishment to avoid potential revenue loss.

---

### Business Value

This dashboard enables business users to:

- Monitor overall sales and profitability.
- Track revenue trends over time.
- Compare regional sales performance.
- Identify top-selling products.
- Analyze category-wise profitability.
- Monitor inventory health.
- Detect low-stock products.
- Make informed inventory and sales decisions using interactive visualizations.
